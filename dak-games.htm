HI-LO

Gary Butler 2016, lake Kokoko, Temagami, Ontario, Canada

(1) Player rolls 5 dice

(2) add up total show on dice
- aiming for either Highest or Lowest score

(3) You get 2 re-rolls, each time for as many dice as you want

(4) add up your dice. Other player goes

(5) Closest to either 5 or 30 wins
(you can change going for Hi or Lo anytime)

EXAMPLE:
Player one scores 2, 1, 4, 3, 1 — 11.
Player two scores 6, 4, 4, 3, 5 — 22
OUTCOME: 11 is closer to 5 than 22 is to 30, so player one wins round

Game to be played on top of a food barrel in the woods. Winner of each round gets 3 sour Ju-Jubes. Loser gets one Ju-Jube. Any show of 5 of a kind awards a full sour candy belt. All rule disputes to be settled by Gary Butler "because I made up the game"
