DATA & NOTES
------------------


The first diagram is based on a diagram from a LinkedIn article by MUHAMMAD AZEEM QURESHI (Mugup).
I made my own version - They had shown the ref direction as South, which I 'corrected' to the norm of North, and then I realized that their method was of course better, as it is much easier (in the N hemisphere) to judge the sun position with reference to noon, when we can all see where it is, as opposed to at midnight, when we cannot see that big bright thing we call the 'Sun'.

Shown the original LinkedIn diagram and than my diagrams versions One and Two, and then my versions for Toronto in mid summer, mid winter and the in-between times. As always, I try to simplify and round-off data and diagrams so that I can 'grasp' them as a general rule rather than having to calculate for individual instance use.
