2004 Raleigh HIGHLANDER
(beater bike)

I had this listed as 1992 for some reason. That is too early. Wheels are 700/622 not 26", and V-brakes did not come around until 1996. Serial # may indicate a 2004, after Raleigh went out of business & was sold to Derby.

Freebee bike being thrown out by a neighbour. I knocked on door to confirm a freebee. Looks bad at first - but frame is AOK, good V-brakes, cool paint job just hidden under those aweful graphic decals, and terrible seat and seat post make it look bad. But it's. good. Wheels are 700/622 but look AOK.

NEEDS/NEEDED

new post & seat, top-mount shifter, likely pedals and tires. Handlebar it has is kinda great, just rusty. New grips of course. Just basics and it will be AOK for sure. Oh - biggest issue is front chainrings are crappy one-peiece - I can't replace the big ring with a solid billit. Hopefully can get cheap rings (and cranks?) to replace - ideally also from a 'trash bike' someone shedding in spring or fall.

GEOMETRY

Top tube is high but bike is 1992 short, so all good.
Fits well. High front may mean can get lower rise (ie cooler looking) stem - & resize bike via that if needed.
Overall the unintentionally cool paint and near level top tube - with short run - make it a cool bike when stripped back. Nad heck the darn V brakes really do work well, just as Rivendell says.
