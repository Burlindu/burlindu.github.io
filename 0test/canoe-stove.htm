MSR WHISPERLIGHT
BRC PDF Instruc sheet
FUEL:
MSR Superfuel, Coleman Fuel (usually 950ml / 30oz)
MSR Bottle is 20 oz / 600 ml
1 Bottle Burns 136 min1 oz Fuel biold 1.5L

USE ESTIMATE:
1-2 oz per person per day
2 persons can go 5-10 days on a Bottle
4 persons can do weekend on a Bottle
(20OZ MSR bottle)

1.
* Leave air space in fuel bottle
20-30 pumps to pressurise
Lubricate brass tip with spit before assembling
secure with rotating clip

2.
1/2 TURN open
Fill primer cup with Fuel

3.
Close valve
light to pre-heat, let burn down

4.
open value to cook heat
Approx 10 pumps every 10 min

FIN.
To turn off close valve
Let Cool!
unscrew to depressurise bottle.

**** EXTRA TIPS ****
- set up on stone table next to small tree and wrap in Helly Hanson rain jacket overnight
- Always use heat shield
- Dent in one corner of heat shield and use the shield as the base - above the flame - for frying pan cooking
- Take several BIC lighters
