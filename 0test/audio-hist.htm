NAD 3020 with Realistic Minmus 7's

Greatest bargain system ($100-150 from Trent TV)

JVC mini system UX-T1
1994-x
I genrally hate min-systems but loves the UX-T1
Distinctive super bright display and full powered remote - even the tape deck and CD lid had full remote control
Included AUX input (for TV), full remote and gentle bass boost - & actual MDF cabinet speakers with front porting
Used landscaping cinder blocks as speaker stands as part of TV AV system in Vancouver (With Panasonic GAOO 27")
Salesperson sold me on this system by playing Star Wars Soundtrack as test CD
add Sony PS-T1 Turntable - turntable prices dropped with arrival of the CD
