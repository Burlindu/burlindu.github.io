- DIV classes are better because they are repeatable
- ZONECLASSWIDE has no right padding to allow 4 square buttons
- ZONECLASS (reg) has right padding so is better for text but bumps down 1 BUTTON
- Square buttons (Class=button1) always have a right margin/padding so they look good when maxed out in zoneclasswide.
- In a regular zoneclass button1 either bumpts down or would have a double margin/padding on the right

So Right margin/padding will look the same / correct if I use button1's in zoneclasswide and text in zoneclass.
I don't know if this is the best coding solution but it works

Usually the format is a zoneclasswide at the top for the big square buttons and than a series of regular zoneclass below for regular content
