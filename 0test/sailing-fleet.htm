Theia is a J100 race-cruiser
So a 10 meter boat (about 33')
with a handicap or PHRF (Performance Handicap Racing Fleet) number of 119
(a 119 means boat would be 119 seconds behind a hypothetical benchmark boat)
Theia is rated as PHRF II start, after the faster 105's start

*** a J100 is 10m, a J105 is 10.5m, a J35 is 35' - some years they used metric, some years imperial
