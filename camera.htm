EXTRA NOTES ON FILM & digital

Film is the best, and digital is the best Too

The Vivitar light box was an amazing zoom-with-your-feet camera - no ficusing, and full 35mm film glory.

The Pentax Super Program was more than a camera, it was a friend. Learning how to guess ditances so you could focus without looking through the viewfinder was awesome.

The Pixel means you don't have to post-process colour. The software ajustments are just unreal. If you can get your favorite FOV you can just treat it like a magic prime, like in the old days

FINALLY - using the 3a to photograph my old Super-Program slides, projected on a white wall, makes for an amazing digital-film hybrid. Pixel software magic combined with film slide 35mm magic.
