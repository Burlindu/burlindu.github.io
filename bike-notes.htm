SEAT POST
SHELDON BROWN SAYS OLD STEEL BIKES MEASURE TUBE DIAMETER ON THE OUTSIDE TYPICAL 1 1/8" (28.6) SEAT TUBE. (SO WHEN TUBES GOT THINNER SEATPOSTS GOT THICKER....)
ALL RALEIGH 3-SPEEDS ARE 1" 25.4mm SEAT POSTS

FRONT WHEEL HUB
**** CHECK THIS RUINS HUB IF INSTALLED WRONG ****
CHECK FRONT WHEEL - SB SAYS THE RALEIGH 3-SPEED FRONT HUB IS DIRECTIONAL & CAN BE INSTALLED BACKWARDS ON THE FORK - CHECK THIS!!

BSD
TIRES USE SHRADER TUBES 26 x 1 3/8".
1 3/8 is 34.9mm (rim width)
ISO/ETRTO ARE STD TIRES/WHEEL SIZING. ISO IS THE BEAD SEAT DIAMETER - NOT THE VISIBLE RIM. IT IS THE SMALLEST DIAMETER OF THE TIRE INSIDE THE RIM. **RIM DIAMETER IS TYPICAL 10MM GREATER THAN BSD.
26 x 1 3/8" E.A.3 is ISO/ETRTO 590mm BSD. FRENCH EQUIV(?) IS 650A TYPE. A 700C WHEEL IS 622mm.

BB
BOTTOM BRACKET IS LIKELY: Raleigh, 1 3/8" X 26 tpi, CUPS R-L, (34.9 x ? mm) HANDLEBARS: OLD ENGLISH 3-SPEED SIZE, CLAMP IS 15/16ths, 23.8mm
GRIP AREA IS 22.2" or 7/8". NEW BARS ARE LIKELY 25.4mm AT THE CLAMP - TOO BIG!! HEADSET: 1" RALEIGH TYPE. STEM IS 22.2mm 7/8", STEERER OUTER DIAMETER 25.4", FRAME CUP 30mm OUTSIDE.
