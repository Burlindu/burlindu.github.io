Page Playoff Format
The page format ranks the teams into 4 seeds in the round robin

Seeds 3 & 4 play to see how gets to advance to the semis

Seeds 1 & 2 play to see who (winner) gets a bye to the finals and who (loser) goes to the semis

Semis - Winner of 3/4 and loser of 1/2 play to see who gets the other spot in the finals

Finals:
Winner of the semis match vs winner of the team that got the bye in the quarters
