My Take
----------------------------------------

I love my Tarn 3. But....
- The tapering interior means by mod standards (20" wide per person) it's not a 3.
- With vestibule open the front opening is not shielded by the fly. That's dumb - The back should have proper venting with the fly down
- If you're going with a full 3-pole setup I would trade off some wind stabiility for better tent housing over the front fly
- Front fly opening should be box rather than split triangle do you can prop it open and get a proper view stright out
